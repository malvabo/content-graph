Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Oula ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

Account information (optional). When you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Your Apple user ID (a stable, app-scoped identifier Apple assigns)
  • Your email address (or an Apple-generated relay address if you use Hide My Email)
  • Your name (only on first sign-in, and only if you share it)

This information is stored on our servers solely to identify your account and associate your data with it. We do not sell, share, or monetise it.

Information that stays on your device. Your notes, voice transcripts, and AI-generated content are stored locally using your device's storage. We do not transmit or store this content on our servers.

Your Anthropic API key is stored securely in your device's Keychain. It is never sent to our servers — it goes directly from your device to Anthropic's API.

How we use your information

  • Authentication: Your Apple user ID lets you log in and keeps your account session active.
  • App functionality: Your email and name (where provided) may be used to contact you about important account or service changes.

We do not use your personal information for advertising, analytics, or tracking.

Third-party services

Anthropic. When you use Oula's AI generation features, the content you provide (your notes, voice transcripts, or text input) is sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API using your own API key. Anthropic's privacy policy governs how they handle this data.

Apple Sign In. Authentication is handled by Apple's Sign in with Apple service. Apple's privacy policy governs that interaction.

Apple Speech Recognition. Voice recording uses Apple's on-device and cloud speech recognition. Apple's privacy policy governs how transcription data is handled.

Data retention

Account data (user ID, email, name) is retained for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us at the address below. Local data (notes, generations) can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data from device settings.

Children's privacy

Oula is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
